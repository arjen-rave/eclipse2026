        Warning

        Never look directly at the sun, even briefly, while using this. Keep your eclipse glasses on at all times — only look at this screen, never along the same line of sight as the camera. This is a “find the sun” aid, not a photography tool — it helps you aim, then hands off to your phone's own camera app for the actual photo, with all its normal settings (exposure, focus, zoom, modes). Your phone's optical zoom won't get you a dramatically better eclipse photo either way — the sun will still appear as a small dot — but this way you at least keep every control you're used to.
